# MalcolmToddDiscography-Archipelago-Manual
An Archipelago manual for Malcolm Todd's discography.

Currently includes:

- All 3 albums. (Do That Again, Malcolm Todd, Sweet Boy)
- Malcolm Todd (still) Deluxe
- Comfort Me (Single)
- Two New Malcolm Todd Songs EP
- Shower Shoes EP
- Demos Before Prom EP

All albums, EPs, and singles have progressive unlocks.

- The progressive album unlocks open up 2 songs each.
- The progressive EP unlocks, deluxe unlocks and single unlocks open up 1 song each.
- There are 2 extra progressive items per album and EP, and 1 extra progressive item for Comfort Me and Two New Malcolm Todd Songs.
- Malcolm Todd (still) will also open up with enough Progressive Malcolm Todd items.

GOAL: Listen to every song. The goal only unlocks when you have all required items to listen to every song.
